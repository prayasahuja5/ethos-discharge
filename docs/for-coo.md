# For Hospital Leadership — Ethos Executive Summary

> **Meds-to-Beds Program: Financial Impact & Expansion Case**
> Saint John's Regional Medical Center · March 2026

---

## The Problem — In One Sentence

Patients who are medically cleared to leave are staying in hospital beds for 4+ hours because of broken coordination between pharmacy, case managers, SNFs, and insurance — and it's costing the hospital $600,000+ per year.

---

## The Core Bottlenecks We're Fixing

These are the five reasons discharge delays happen — and what Ethos does about each:

| Bottleneck | Current State | Ethos Solution |
|-----------|--------------|---------------|
| **SNF placement** | Manual calls to facilities, avg 28-hour wait | Auto-referral to ranked facilities + 30-min escalation if no response |
| **Pharmacy notification** | 45–90 min manual fax gap after discharge order | Face sheet auto-sent to pharmacy the moment discharge order is signed |
| **PCP assignment** | 80% of patients lack a PCP, no standardized workflow | Auto-match to insurance-compatible clinics at admission |
| **Ownership clarity** | No clear assignment of who handles what | Case manager auto-assigned at admission by floor and workload |
| **Post-discharge follow-up** | Lost patients, missed billing opportunities | Automated 24h / 7-day / 30-day call schedule |

## The Savings Opportunity

If Ethos reduces nonmedical delay by just **50%** across all 800 monthly discharges:

| Scenario | Hours Saved / Month | Monthly Savings | Annual Savings |
|----------|-------------------|----------------|---------------|
| 25% delay reduction | ~900 hours | **$121,500** | **$1.46M** |
| 50% delay reduction | ~1,760 hours | **$237,600** | **$2.85M** |
| 75% delay reduction | ~2,640 hours | **$356,400** | **$4.28M** |

> These projections use the same $135/hr rate and observed 4.4h avg delay, scaled to 800 patients.

**Additionally**: Earlier discharge = more available beds = more admissions. Each recovered bed day represents potential additional revenue from patients currently being diverted or delayed.

## What We're Asking For

1. **Read-only Epic FHIR integration** — replaces the manual ADT message simulator with real-time patient data. Mohammed has confirmed the API specification is available.

2. **Case manager workflow adoption** — 30-minute onboarding session per case manager. The tool is designed around their existing workflow, not replacing it.

3. **Continued data sharing** — monthly discharge Excel exports for Ethos Pulse analytics to track improvement over time.
